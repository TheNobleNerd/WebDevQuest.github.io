Welcome To The Quest!

Hello and welcome to Web Dev Quest! My name is JR. I am a husband, father and USMC veteran on a quest of obtaining the knowledge and skills required to become a web developer. The purpose of this website is to serve as a guide to all who seek to reaching the same goal.

As you navigate through the site you will see that I have documented, in detail, the steps that I have taken as well as the mistakes that I've made along my journey. In addition I will provide links to any documentation and tutorials that have aided me in my journey.

I will try my best to provide as much free material as possible. However, some of the resources that I've used do require payment. That being said, I will do what I can to find any free options that server as equal alternatives. If you have any questions or sugestions, please feel free to DM me on twitter @JR_Noble1. Thank you for visiting my blog and I hope that you chose to join me on this quest of becoming a Web Developer!
